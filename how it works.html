How it works

A sense of safety in 3 simple steps

01

Purchase SAFE door alarm

Never again feel undefended within the bounds of your own home! Wait for the SAFE door alarm to be delivered to your home and it will provide the security you're looking for!

02

Set up your SAFE door alarm

Unbox the door alarm components from the package. Simply add the magnets from the package to the top or side of the front-facing part your door and fix the Micro:bit, with all the components connected, next to the magnets on the doorframe or wall.

03

Sit back and enjoy

With the SAFE door alarm set up, you can now relax and relish in your peace of mind as the door alarm will stave off any potential dangers to you and your home while supplying you with a constant activity feed which can be viewed directly from the website.
