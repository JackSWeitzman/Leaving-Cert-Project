The Product

Breakdown of our product

Main Features

The main feature of the device is having it connected to a door or window with a magnetic strength detection system that triggers an alarm when magnetic strength is too low. This is caused by the magnet being moved away from the device.

Other features of the product include the ability to attach the device to a door or window, turn the magnetic detection on or off, monitor the device activity in the 'Live Feed' tab and visual responsiveness on the device screen and with two LEDs.

Problem Being Addressed

The problem being addressed is the lack of safety found in homes or the worry of what might happen in the home when the consumer is not currently there. This lack of safety can have a mental, physical and emotional impact on homeowners and residents of a house.

Solution To The Problem

We strived to solve this problem by creating a reliable and simple smart security system that will grant a sense of safety to consumers. We aim to soothe worries of consumers by having an alarm trigger at their home or by having the state of the system be accessible from anywhere.
